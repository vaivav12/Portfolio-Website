The Evolution of Tech

My journey through the digital landscape

I have always been deeply fascinated by technology and its multifaceted nature. Born in 2006, I belong to a generation that didn't just witness the digital revolution—we were born into it. From the early days of bulky desktops to the sleek, AI-integrated era we live in today, I have evolved alongside tech, watching it grow, adapt, and transform the world around us. This lifelong exposure has sparked an insatiable curiosity in me to understand not just how to use tools, but how they work fundamentally.

In fact, the inception of this very website is a testament to that journey. It all started with a simple observation: seeing others use Linktree pages to consolidate their online presence. Inspired, I created my own, but my ambition quickly outgrew the template's limitations. I wanted something more personal, more expressive—a true digital home. Driven by the desire to have a portfolio website that stood out like the ones I admired, I embarked on learning web development, and hence, here I am, sharing my corner of the internet with you.
