Here's a list of books that I have read and found interesting.

Gunaho Ka Devta

Novel

A classic Hindi novel set in Allahabad, telling the passionate and tragic love story of Chander and Sudha against the backdrop of societal norms and the caste system.

13 Reasons Why

Young Adult Fiction

Clay Jensen receives a box of cassette tapes from his classmate Hannah Baker after her suicide, in which she details the thirteen reasons why she decided to end her life.

You Have Reached Sam

Young Adult Fiction

After her boyfriend Sam dies, Julie calls his phone number to hear his voicemail, but to her shock, he answers. They are given a second chance at goodbye.

It Ends with Us

Romance Novel

Lily Bloom moves to Boston and falls for Ryle Kincaid, but when her first love Atlas Corrigan reappears, she must make a difficult choice that will define her future.

It Starts with Us

Romance Novel

Picking up right where the first book ended, this sequel alternates between Lily and Atlas's perspectives as they navigate their rekindled romance.
