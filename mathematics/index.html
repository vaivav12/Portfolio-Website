The Beauty of Numbers

Exploring the language of the universe

My journey with mathematics has always been deeply rooted in logic and curiosity. I was fortunate to have excellent math teachers who taught me not just the formulas, but the essence of mathematical thinking. Because of this strong foundation, I've always felt a natural connection to the subject.

In my junior classes, this passion led me to win the School Math Olympiad multiple times. It wasn't just about competing; it was about the thrill of solving complex problems.

Now, I am pursuing my B.Sc., where Mathematics is one of my major subjects. I am very keen on mathematics academia. I don't just accept theorems as they are; I always question why this happened and seek to understand every step of the derivation deeply.

Be it Calculus or Differential Equations, I have deep dived into them all. My favorite topics, however, remain Calculus and Probability. I find them fascinating because, much like in Chess, solving an integration problem often requires you to think 3 steps ahead to find the solution.

While I have mastered most of these fields, I am currently working on improving my grasp of Geometry. It remains on my to-do list, and I strictly believe in conquering every aspect of the subject.
