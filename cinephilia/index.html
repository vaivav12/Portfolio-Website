Here's a list of web series and movies that I have watched and found interesting.

The Shawshank Redemption

Movie

Two imprisoned men bond over a number of years, finding solace and eventual redemption through acts of common decency.

Prison Break

Web Series

Due to a political conspiracy, an innocent man is sent to death row and his only hope is his brother, who makes it his mission to deliberately get himself sent to the same prison in order to break the both of them out.

Money Heist (La Casa de Papel)

Web Series

An unusual group of robbers attempt to carry out the most perfect robbery in Spanish history - stealing 2.4 billion euros from the Royal Mint of Spain.

Breaking Bad

Web Series

A high school chemistry teacher diagnosed with inoperable lung cancer turns to manufacturing and selling methamphetamine in order to secure his family's future.

Better Call Saul

Web Series

The trials and tribulations of criminal lawyer Jimmy McGill in the time before he established his strip-mall law office in Albuquerque, New Mexico.

Harry Potter

Movie Franchise

An orphaned boy enrolls in a school of wizardry, where he learns the truth about himself, his family and the terrible evil that haunts the magical world.

Dexter

Web Series

He's smart. He's lovable. He's Dexter Morgan, America's favorite serial killer, who spends his days solving crimes and nights committing them.

Game of Thrones

Web Series

Nine noble families fight for control over the lands of Westeros, while an ancient enemy returns after being dormant for millennia.

House of the Dragon

Web Series

An internal succession war within House Targaryen at the height of its power, 172 years before the birth of Daenerys Targaryen.

Dexter: New Blood & Resurrection

Web Series

New Blood: Ten years after faking his death, Dexter Morgan has moved to Iron Lake, New York, hiding his identity under the name of Jim Lindsay.

Pirates of the Caribbean

Movie Franchise

Blacksmith Will Turner teams up with eccentric pirate "Captain" Jack Sparrow to save his love, the governor's daughter, from Jack's former pirate allies, who are now undead.

Never Have I Ever

Web Series

After a traumatic year, all an Indian-American teen wants is to go from pariah to popular -- but friends, family and feelings won't make it easy on her.

Succession

Web Series

The Roy family is known for controlling the biggest media and entertainment company in the world. However, their world changes when their father steps down from the company.

Gutar Gu

Web Series

A teenage romance drama that explores the nuances of first love, small-town romance, and the complexities of relationships.

Hostel Daze

Web Series

Captures the life of four friends in an engineering hostel, exploring their friendships, conflicts, and the amusing struggle to survive college life.

Aspirants

Web Series

Follows the journey of three friends who are UPSC aspirants in Old Rajinder Nagar, Delhi, highlighting their struggle, friendship, and ambition.

Kota Factory

Web Series

Set in Kota, this show revolves around the life of IIT-JEE aspirants and the intense academic culture of the city's coaching centers.

Cubicles

Web Series

A relatable story about a first-jobber in the corporate world, dealing with deadlines, office politics, and the highs and lows of working in a cubicle.

Immature

Web Series

A coming-of-age story about a group of school friends navigating their first crush, friendship, and the awkwardness of adolescence.
